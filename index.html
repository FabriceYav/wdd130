Lubumbashi

The city of Lubumbashi is located in the southeastern Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC). Capital of the Haut-Katanga province. It is near the border with Zambia, approximately 20 km from it. It is an important hub for rail (line to the port Matadi via Kolwezi and Kinshasa) and road transport, as well as a major croassing point to Zambia (particularly via Kasumbalesa).
